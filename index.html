Transportation

Baton Rouge Trolleys

We have reserved a trolley on the wedding day to shuttle guests from The Cook Hotel to Sacred Heart for the ceremony, and also from the ceremony to the reception.

The trolley will depart The Cook Hotel at 5:30pm to bring guests to Sacred Heart for the ceremony. As the trolley will only make one run prior to the ceremony, there will be limited seating for this trip—the trolley holds approximately 30 guests.

Following the ceremony, the trolley will run between Sacred Heart and Lod Cook for the reception until all guests that need a ride have been accomodated.
